These projects influenced the design and goals of Hiro League.

## Personal AI Infrastructure

[Personal AI Infrastructure](https://github.com/danielmiessler/Personal_AI_Infrastructure?tab=readme-ov-file) by Daniel Miessler — a well-structured approach to running AI locally with personal data ownership. Highly recommended reading.

## OpenClaw and alternatives

[OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) is a personal automation platform with a similar philosophy to Hiro League. It also influenced the choice of Mintlify for documentation.

A broader list of alternatives in the same space: [50 OpenClaw alternatives](https://www.reddit.com/r/AI_Agents/comments/1raq9qy/50_openclaw_alternatives_for_business/) and [ClawClones](https://clawclones.com/).

## Other projects worth watching

- [Ouroboros](https://github.com/razzant/ouroboros?tab=readme-ov-file) — self-improving agent loop concept.
